Available Classes

The Computer Science department at West Chester University is proud to offer cost-free, high-quality tutoring in many computer science classes. Classes offered include:

  • CSC110: Fundamentals of Computer Science
  • CSC115: Introduction to Computer Programming (Visual Basic)
  • CSC141: Computer Science I (Java programming)
  • CSC142: Computer Science II (Java programming)
  • CSW131: Introduction to Web Design


How To Sign Up

If you would like to sign up for a tutor, please contact the Computer Science department secretary, Ms. Georgiana White, at:
Gwhite@wcupa.edu

Please include your name, the class you need tutoring for, and the times that you are available.

She will then contact our tutors to try to find one to meet your needs, and email you back.

If you are unable to find a tutor, try the Learning Assistance and Resource Center.
